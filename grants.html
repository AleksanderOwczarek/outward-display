Research Grants and Funding

I have been fortunate to have had outstanding success in securing national competitive grants. Since 2001, I have been a Chief Investigator (CI) on grant funding in excess of $18 million. I was a Theme Leader and CI in the Asutralian Research Council (ARC) Centre of Excellence for Mathematics and Statistics of Complex Systems for over a decade. Notably, I was one of the first researchers to gain three successive ARC personal fellowships.

Grants funding

  • ARC Discovery Grant, 2023-2025: DP230100674, Transformative simulation techniques for complex polymer networks
  • ARC Discovery Grant, 2016-2020: DP160103562, Interplay of Topology and Geometry in Polymeric Critical Phenomena
  • ARC Discovery Grant, 2012-2014: DP120101593, Advanced numerical and analytical techniques for exact studies in combinatorics and statistical mechanics
